It has been 20 years - what’s next?

The master plan 2022 is a celebration of my personal 20 birthdays. Reaching this milestone means graduating and becoming an experienced (mathematical) statistician. I am therefore improving my lifestyle as preparation for upcoming challenges and launching a few programmes as my legacy to the communities I am currently engaging.
